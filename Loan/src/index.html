The table shows the average interest rate, debt ratio, annual income, and loan amount by each state under each reason category.
In the LendingClub Platform, it lists 14 reason categories for people getting a personal loan with this company. These reasons are as follows, debt_consolidation, credit_card, house, car, other, vacation, home_improvement, small_business, major_purchase, medical, renewable_energy, moving, wedding, educational. In this data visualization,the trend in the past years show that the personal loan amount is more averagely distributed throughout all the reason categories. In the more recent years, from 2009 to 2018, the personal loan amounts are more congregated at certain reason categories. The top three reasons on why Americans get personal loans in the LendingClub Platform from 2009-2018 are: debt_consolidation, credit_card, and home_improvement. This result can show the spending habit for Americans that borrow money just so they could return money from another loan.
